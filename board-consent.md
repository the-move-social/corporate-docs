# Board Consent in Lieu of First Meeting

## The Move Social, Inc.

*A Delaware Corporation*
*A Wholly Owned Subsidiary of Present Tense Labs, Inc.*

**Date:** _________, 2026

The undersigned, being all of the directors of The Move Social, Inc. (the "Corporation"), hereby consent to and adopt the following resolutions in lieu of holding the first meeting of the Board of Directors of the Corporation, pursuant to Section 141(f) of the Delaware General Corporation Law:

---

### 1. Adoption of Bylaws

**RESOLVED**, that the Bylaws attached hereto are hereby adopted as the Bylaws of the Corporation.

### 2. Sole Stockholder

**RESOLVED**, that the Board acknowledges that Present Tense Labs, Inc. is the sole stockholder of the Corporation, having subscribed for shares pursuant to the Stock Subscription Agreement dated _________, 2026.

### 3. Appointment of Officers

**RESOLVED**, that the following persons are hereby appointed as officers of the Corporation, each to serve at the pleasure of the Board until a successor is duly appointed:

| Officer | Name |
|---------|------|
| Co-Chief Executive Officer | Altin Lecaj |
| Co-Chief Executive Officer | Eric Garcia |
| Secretary | Altin Lecaj and Eric Garcia (jointly) |
| Treasurer | Altin Lecaj and Eric Garcia (jointly) |

### 4. Designation of Principal Office

**RESOLVED**, that the principal office of the Corporation shall be located at:

**10 River Rd Apt 10C, New York, NY 10044**

### 5. Fiscal Year

**RESOLVED**, that the fiscal year of the Corporation shall end on December 31 of each year.

### 6. Authorization of Stock Issuance

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation is hereby authorized to issue shares of its capital stock as follows:

| Recipient | Shares | Class | Par Value | Total Consideration |
|-----------|--------|-------|-----------|-------------------|
| Present Tense Labs, Inc. | 8,500,000 | Class A Common Stock (Voting) | $0.00001 | $85.00 |

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that 1,500,000 shares of Class B Common Stock (Non-Voting) are hereby reserved for issuance under the Corporation's Equity Incentive Plan, to be adopted by separate resolution.

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that upon receipt of the consideration specified above, the officers of the Corporation are authorized and directed to issue the shares to Present Tense Labs, Inc.

### 7. Equity Incentive Plan

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation hereby adopts the Equity Incentive Plan in the form attached hereto, reserving 1,500,000 shares of Class B Common Stock (Non-Voting) (15% of the initial plan) for issuance pursuant to stock options, restricted stock, and other equity awards granted under the Plan.

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the Board of Directors shall administer the Plan and is authorized to grant awards thereunder in its discretion.

### 8. IP License

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation is authorized to enter into the Intellectual Property License Agreement with Present Tense Labs, Inc. in substantially the form attached hereto, pursuant to which the Corporation will receive a license to certain technology and intellectual property owned by Present Tense Labs, Inc.

### 9. Intercompany Services Agreement

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation is authorized to enter into the Intercompany Services Agreement with Present Tense Labs, Inc. in substantially the form attached hereto.

### 10. Bank Account

**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized to open a bank account in the name of the Corporation at a financial institution of their choosing, and to execute such documents as may be required by the institution.

### 11. Employer Identification Number

**RESOLVED**, that the officers are authorized and directed to apply for a federal Employer Identification Number (EIN) from the Internal Revenue Service and to file any required state tax registrations.

### 12. Qualification to Do Business

**RESOLVED**, that the officers are authorized and directed to qualify the Corporation to do business in any state where such qualification is required.

### 13. Expenses

**RESOLVED**, that the officers are authorized to pay all expenses incurred in connection with the organization and incorporation of the Corporation.

### 14. Omnibus Resolution

**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are authorized and directed to take any and all actions and execute any and all documents necessary or appropriate to carry out the purposes and intent of the foregoing resolutions.
