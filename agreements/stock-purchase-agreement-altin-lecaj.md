## Recitals

A. The Company has been incorporated under the laws of the State of Delaware with authority to issue 10,000,000 shares of Common Stock, par value $0.00001 per share.

B. The Purchaser serves as Chief Executive Officer and as a director of the Company.

C. The Company desires to sell, and the Purchaser desires to purchase, shares of the Company's Common Stock, subject to the vesting and repurchase terms set forth herein, in order to align the Purchaser's interests with those of the Company.

D. Upon consummation of this purchase and the concurrent purchase by Eric Garcia of an equal number of shares, the Purchaser will hold 50% of the issued and outstanding shares of the Company.

---

## 1. Purchase and Sale

### 1.1 Sale of Shares

Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, the Company hereby sells to the Purchaser, and the Purchaser hereby purchases from the Company, **5,000,000 shares** of the Company's **Common Stock**, par value $0.00001 per share (the "Shares").

### 1.2 Purchase Price

The aggregate purchase price for the Shares shall be **$50.00** ($0.00001 per share) (the "Purchase Price"), payable to the Company in cash, check, or wire transfer upon execution of this Agreement.

### 1.3 Payment

Payment of the Purchase Price is due upon execution of this Agreement. The Shares shall not be issued until the Company has received payment in full.

---

## 2. Vesting

### 2.1 Vesting Commencement Date

The vesting commencement date shall be _________________ (the date of this Agreement) (the "Vesting Commencement Date").

### 2.2 Vesting Schedule

The Shares shall vest according to the following schedule:

| Milestone | Shares Vested | Cumulative |
|-----------|--------------|------------|
| 1st anniversary of Vesting Commencement Date | 25% of total Shares (1,250,000) | 25% |
| Each month thereafter for 36 months | 1/48 of total Shares (~104,167) per month | 25% to 100% |

### 2.3 Cliff

No Shares shall vest before the first anniversary of the Vesting Commencement Date (the "Cliff"). If the Purchaser's service with the Company terminates before the first anniversary, no Shares shall have vested and all Shares shall be subject to the Company's repurchase right under Section 3.

### 2.4 Continued Service Required

Vesting requires the Purchaser's continued service to the Company as an officer, director, employee, or consultant. Vesting shall cease immediately upon termination of all service relationships between the Purchaser and the Company.

---

## 3. Company Repurchase Right

### 3.1 Repurchase Right

Upon termination of the Purchaser's service with the Company for any reason (whether voluntary or involuntary, with or without cause), the Company shall have the right, but not the obligation, to repurchase any or all of the Shares that have not yet vested as of the date of termination (the "Unvested Shares") at the original Purchase Price of $0.00001 per share (the "Repurchase Price").

### 3.2 Exercise Period

The Company must exercise the repurchase right by delivering written notice to the Purchaser (or the Purchaser's estate or legal representative) within ninety (90) days following the date of termination of service (the "Repurchase Period"). The closing of the repurchase shall occur within ten (10) days after delivery of such notice.

### 3.3 Lapse of Repurchase Right

If the Company does not exercise the repurchase right within the Repurchase Period, the repurchase right shall lapse with respect to those Unvested Shares, and such Shares shall thereafter be free of the repurchase restriction (but shall remain subject to all other restrictions under this Agreement).

### 3.4 Termination Upon Change of Control

The Company's repurchase right under this Section 3 shall terminate immediately upon the consummation of a Change of Control. For purposes of this Agreement, "Change of Control" means (a) a merger or consolidation of the Company with or into another entity in which the stockholders of the Company immediately prior to such transaction do not hold a majority of the voting power of the surviving entity, (b) a sale of all or substantially all of the Company's assets, or (c) a transaction or series of related transactions in which a person or group acquires more than 50% of the Company's outstanding voting stock.

### 3.5 Escrow of Unvested Shares

As security for the Company's repurchase right, the Purchaser agrees that the Unvested Shares shall be held in escrow by the Company (or its designee) until such Shares vest or are repurchased. The Purchaser shall execute any documents reasonably required to effectuate such escrow arrangement.

## 4. Section 83(b) Election

### 4.1 Opportunity to File

The Purchaser acknowledges that the Purchaser has been informed of the opportunity to file an election under Section 83(b) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended (the "Code"), with the Internal Revenue Service within thirty (30) days of the date of this Agreement.

### 4.2 Company Assistance

The Company shall provide the Purchaser with a pre-filled Section 83(b) election form (substantially in the form attached as Exhibit B) and written instructions for filing such election with the Internal Revenue Service.

### 4.3 Proof of Filing

The Purchaser shall deliver to the Company a copy of the filed Section 83(b) election and proof of timely filing (such as a certified mail receipt) within thirty-five (35) days of the date of this Agreement.

### 4.4 Acknowledgment of Tax Consequences

The Purchaser acknowledges that failure to file a timely Section 83(b) election may result in adverse tax consequences to the Purchaser, including recognition of ordinary income as the Shares vest. The Company makes no representations regarding the tax treatment of this purchase and the Purchaser has been advised to consult with the Purchaser's own tax advisor.
