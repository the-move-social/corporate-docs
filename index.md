# The Move Social, Inc.

**Delaware C-Corp — Wholly Owned Subsidiary of Present Tense Labs, Inc.**

The Move Social operates **The Move**, a social adventure app that turns real-world meetups into AI-generated quests. It is the first subsidiary of [Present Tense Labs](https://docs.presenttenselabs.com).

## Relationship to Parent

PTL licenses core technology to TMS under the [IP License Agreement](ip-license-agreement.md):
- Reputation engine and scoring algorithms
- Vector matching infrastructure
- Experience generation models
- Safety layer
- Shared brand assets and infrastructure

TMS operates under license. The core tech stays at PTL.

## Team Equity

TMS has a 15% stock option pool (1,500,000 Class B shares) for team members joining on an equity basis. Options vest over 4 years with a 1-year cliff.

If you're joining the team, read the [Equity Explainer](equity-explainer.md) — it explains your options, protections, and what to expect in plain language.
